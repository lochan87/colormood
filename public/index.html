About ColorMood

Mental wellness meets generative AI

What is ColorMood?

ColorMood is an innovative mental wellness application that combines the power of AI with creative expression. By simply describing your mood, you'll receive personalized abstract art, curated music playlists, and thoughtful journaling prompts designed to help you process and understand your emotions.

AI Mood Analysis

Advanced AI analyzes your emotional state and identifies key patterns, triggers, and intensity levels.

Personalized Art

Generate unique abstract art that visually represents your emotional state using colors and forms.

Curated Playlists

Discover music recommendations that match your mood and help you process your emotions.

Guided Reflection

Receive thoughtful journaling prompts tailored to your emotional state and personal growth.

Privacy & Security

Your emotional data is precious to us. All mood entries are associated with anonymous session IDs, and no personal information is stored. Your data is used solely to provide personalized recommendations and improve your experience.
